BARBOUR BRBR (BRBR)

Whitepaper — Global Youth. Global Hope.

Mission: Anti-Bullying • Mental Health Support • Youth Development
Core values: Courage • Compassion • Community

1. Vision

Barbour BRBR is a global movement born from silence and transformed into strength. The project exists to fight bullying, moral harassment, abuse, and fear — while empowering youth worldwide through story, support, education, and community.

2. Why it matters

  • Bullying destroys confidence and mental health.
  • Many victims stay silent due to fear, shame, or lack of support.
  • BRBR turns a real-life story into a global message: speak up, rebuild, help others.

3. The Token (BRBR)
